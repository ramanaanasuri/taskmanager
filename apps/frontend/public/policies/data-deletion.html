How to Delete Your Data

If you have used Facebook Login to access Task Manager and wish to delete your data, you have two options:

Method 1: Delete from Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "Task Manager-AWS" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke access and delete your data

Method 2: Email Us

Send an email to: a_ramanakumar@hotmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Task Manager

Include:

  • Your Facebook name or email
  • Confirmation: "I request deletion of all my data"

Processing Time: Within 30 days
